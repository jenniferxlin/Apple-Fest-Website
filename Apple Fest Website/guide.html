FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

  1. When and where is Apple Fest this year?
    The 35th Annual Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival is from September 29th to October 1st at Ithaca Commons Pedestrian Mall, 202 East State St., Ithaca.
  2. How much does it cost?
    The festival is open to all who want to come. Free admission! However, if you may bring money to buy from any of the food and craft vendors.
  3. How do you get there?
    The TCAT bus provides many routes you could take to the Commons.
    Cornell freshmen living on North Campus, board Route 30 at Risley Hall.
    Cornell upperclassmen living on West Campus, board Route 31 at Baker Flagpole.
    Those living in Collegetown, board Route 10 at Schwartz Performing Arts Center.
  4. If I drive, where can I park?
    Parking for the festival will be available in the Green, Seneca and Cayuga street garages for $5.
  5. What should I wear/bring?
    Dress appropriately according to the weather that day. Because there is a lot of walking around, browsing, and exploring involved, it is best to wear comfortable shoes. You can also bring a water bottle to keep yourself hydrated and a bag to carry all the free things you'll collect and items you buy!
  6. Should I go? Is this event popular?
    It's become an annual celebration for a reason. We had a turnout of over 35,000 people last year!
